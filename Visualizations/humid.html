Humidity


High levels of humidity appear not to be unique to any specific latitude, but it is more common at latitudes closer to the equator. As we head away from the equator, we see a larger variation in humidity across cities. Interestingly, as we move past 50 degrees north, we see almost all cities experiencing humidty of 60% or higher.
